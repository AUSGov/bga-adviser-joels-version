Victorian State Government

Gippsland Business Connect

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

Gippsland Business Connect provides businesses and start-ups in the Latrobe Valley area with free, flexible, confidential and adaptable tailored support to connect them to the various services and information relevant to their business.

Services offered include:

  • tailored and personalised support
  • access to up-to-date information and new opportunities
  • connections to the most appropriate services
  • a central point of contact to direct enquiries.

Concierge services are available in the following local government areas:

  • Latrobe City
  • Wellington Shire
  • Baw Baw Shire
  • Bass Coast Shire
  • South Gippsland Shire
  • East Gippsland Shire.
  • Delivery type: Face to face, Mobile services, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: Latrobe Valley
