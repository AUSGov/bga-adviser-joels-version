Federal Government

Industry Capability Network

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

The Industry Capability Network (ICN) provides Australian businesses with access to a powerful online tool to help connect them with suitable suppliers, project managers and business opportunities across Australia and New Zealand.

Australian or New Zealand companies can register on the ICN Gateway for free. Once registered, they will be able to:

  • browse their online database
  • express interest in work
  • receive notifications.

Registered companies may also be recommended to project owners when appropriate.

Joining the network can offer businesses access to:

  • projects large and small
  • partnership opportunities and supply chain development
  • channels to promote their business capabilities through the network.

Industry Capability Network aims to put project developers in contact with the best suppliers and suppliers in contact with the best projects across Australia, New Zealand and around the world.

There are offices covering every state and territory of Australia.

  • Delivery type: Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: Australia wide.
