Federal Government

Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade Customised Statistics

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

The Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade can provide businesses with up-to-date trade and investment statistics to help them make informed decisions relating to their business.

They can provide detailed trade and investment data on Australia and the rest of the world and can also provide general economic and socioeconomic statistics for a number of countries.

The data is accessed from a number of sources, including:

  • Australia's official trade records
  • trade records from more than 200 other countries and regions, covering more than 90 per cent of worldwide trade
  • statistics sourced from more than 100 offices and bodies across the world.
  • Delivery type: Phone services
  • Service area: Australia wide
