Entrepreneurship Facilitators

Entrepreneurship Facilitators

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

Entrepreneurship Facilitators offer a free service to help people who want to start and run their own business and create their own job.

Entrepreneurship Facilitators:

  • promote and encourage self-employment by providing information and advice about starting and running a business
  • provide tailored mentoring and advice through one-on-one meetings, workshops and networking events
  • refer people to other services that will help them start and run their own business, including New Business Assistance with NEIS.

Facilitators operate in 23 locations across Australia. Anyone wanting to start their own business can access services from an Entrepreneurship Facilitator in these locations.

  • Service area: Australia wide
