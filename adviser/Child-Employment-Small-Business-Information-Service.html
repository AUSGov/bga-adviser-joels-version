Victorian State Government

Child Employment Small Business Information Service

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

The Child Employment Small Business Information Service (CESBIS) provides small businesses across Victoria with free advice about child employment laws.

CESBIS offers one-on-one information and advice regarding a business's responsibilities and obligations under the Victorian Child Employment Act 2003 when employing children.

  • Delivery type: Face to face, Phone services
  • Service area: All of Victoria.
