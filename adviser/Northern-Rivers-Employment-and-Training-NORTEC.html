Northern Rivers Employment and Training NORTEC

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

NORTEC Small Business Solutions provides small businesses in northern NSW and south eastern QLD with low-cost advice, support and training to help them build and develop sustainable businesses.

They use locally employed business advisers who understand the challenges small businesses face in these areas.

Business advisers deliver a large-range of low-cost business and educational services, including:

  • business planning
  • diagnostic business assessment
  • achieving economic sustainability
  • workshops
  • training

NORTEC also offers access to the New Enterprise Incentive Scheme (NEIS).

NORTEC has over 40 offices located across northern NSW and south east Queensland. They also have mobile advisers available to travel to your business.

  • Delivery type: Face to face, Mobile services, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: The Northern Rivers region of NSW and the Gold Coast region of Queensland
