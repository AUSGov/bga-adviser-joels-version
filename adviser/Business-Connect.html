Business Connect

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

Business Connect advisers provide practical support to small business owners, to help them make the right decisions for their business.

The advisers are accredited professionals with first-hand experience running their own small business. Their advice is independent and completely confidential.

Business advice covers business-related topics including:

  • managing cash flow and accessing finance
  • marketing to reach new customers
  • implementing digital tools such as ecommerce
  • developing a business or recovery plan
  • managing change
  • innovating
  • building business capability to supply goods or services to government
  • connecting to other support including government programs and professional services.
  • Delivery type: Face to face, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: NSW
