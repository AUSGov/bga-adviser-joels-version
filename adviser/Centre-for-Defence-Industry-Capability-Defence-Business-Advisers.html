Centre for Defence Industry Capability

Centre for Defence Industry Capability Defence Business Advisers

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

The Centre for Defence Industry Capability helps small to medium sized businesses improve their capability to work with Defence and take advantage of opportunities in the defence industry.

Our national network of advisers can help your business with:

  • business management
  • skills development
  • innovation opportunities
  • access to export initiatives and international and domestic supply chains.

Eligible businesses can access grants to implement recommendations made through our advisory and facilitation services.

  • Delivery type: Face to face, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: Australia wide
