New South Wales Government

NSW Small Business Commissioner

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

The New South Wales Small Business Commission (NSWSBC) provides NSW small businesses with resources, strategic advice and low-cost mediation services.

The NSWSBC can help with information about:

  • business disputes and mediation
  • COVID-19
  • retail and commercial leases
  • advice for building and construction subcontractors
  • financial hardship
  • small business bushfire and drought support
  • starting and running a home business
  • closing a business
  • retail security bond scheme
  • Delivery type: Face to face, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: All of New South Wales.
