Queensland State Government

WorkCover Queensland

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

WorkCover Queensland provides Queensland small businesses with free work, health and safety advisory services.

Businesses can have a one-on-one advisory visit from a WorkCover consultant to get advice on:

  • health and safety issues at your workplace
  • how to develop and implement a safety management system
  • devising strategies and solutions to manage work, health and safety hazards.

Business networks or linked groups (such as franchises, co-located businesses) can request a safety workshop to cover a range of topics, including:

  • basic safety management systems
  • risk management
  • safe work procedures
  • hazardous manual tasks
  • hazardous chemicals
  • injury management and return to work.
  • Delivery type: Face to face, Mobile services, Online services
  • Service area: All of Queensland.
