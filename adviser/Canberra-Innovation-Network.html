Australian Capital Territory Government

Canberra Innovation Network

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

The Canberra Innovation Network provides entrepreneurs and SMEs in the ACT with access to leading experts, programs, events and advice to help them turn their ideas into high impact, commercial realities.

Free 30-minute consultations are available to help you with:

  • business insights and tips to avoid common pitfalls
  • relevant funding opportunities, and additional pathways to capital
  • introductions to existing networks of like-minded innovators, and connections with experts and mentors.
  • Delivery type: Face to face, Phone services
  • Service area: Australia wide
