Greater Shepparton Business Centre

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

Greater Shepparton Business Centre provides small business operators and intenders with access to a range of services, including:

  • guidance and mentoring
  • subsidised premises
  • meeting rooms for hire
  • general business advice
  • training
  • networking
  • workshops.
  • Delivery type: Face to face, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: Shepparton region of Victoria, Mooroopna, Tatura, Murchison, Dookie.
