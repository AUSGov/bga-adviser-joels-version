Northern Territory Government

Small Business Champions

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

If you are a Northern Territory business, Aboriginal enterprise or not-for-profit, you can get help from a small business champion.

A small business champion can help you identify the types of support your business can access.

You can also get support with networking, contacts, information, tools and resources.

Small business champions are located in:

  • Berrimah
  • Darwin
  • Nhulunbuy
  • Katherine
  • Tenant Creek
  • Alice Springs.
  • Delivery type: Face to face, Phone services
  • Service area: Northern Territory.
