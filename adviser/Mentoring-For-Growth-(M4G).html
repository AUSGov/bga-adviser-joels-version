Queensland State Government

Mentoring For Growth (M4G)

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

Mentoring for Growth (M4G) provides Queensland businesses with the opportunity to engage with experienced business mentors who can help them with any challenges or opportunities facing their business.

Businesses wishing to access M4G advice must have:

  • an Australian business number (ABN) and be registered for goods and services tax (GST)
  • Queensland headquarters or significant Queensland operations
  • a business plan in place
  • commenced business operations.

Mentor chats are available via Skype, face-to-face or at Queensland government events across Queensland.

  • Delivery type: Face to face, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: All of Queensland.
