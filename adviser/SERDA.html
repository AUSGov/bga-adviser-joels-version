SERDA

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

SERDA operates in Tasmania's South East, and provides free workforce development assistance, business advisory services, training and job placement for local people and local businesses as part of Enterprise Centres Tasmania.

Find help with:

  • starting or expanding a business
  • managing your cash flow
  • dealing with unforeseen events
  • developing a marketing strategy
  • taking your business online
  • commercialising an idea
  • exporting your product
  • identifying new opportunities and networks.
  • Delivery type: Face to face, Phone services
  • Service area: South-east region of Tasmania.
