Federal Government

JobAccess

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

JobAccess provides businesses with access to free information about supporting and employing people with a disability.

Through the web and phone service, employers can access information on:

  • reasonable adjustments and workplace modifications
  • financial support that may be available
  • disclosure of disability
  • mental health conditions at work
  • creating a disability-confident workplace
  • disability employment case studies, tools and checklists.
  • Delivery type: Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: Australia wide.
