Queensland State Government

Office of the Queensland Small Business Commissioner (OQSBC)

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

The Office of the Queensland Small Business Commissioner provides advocacy support to Queensland based small businesses.

The OQSBC helps small business owners, small business lease tenants and landlords by:

  • Providing information and advisory services to small businesses in Queensland about matters relevant to them.
  • Assisting small businesses with negotiating commercial rent relief during COVID-19. This includes providing initial information and advisory services, and pre-mediation services to connect tenants and landlords to try to identify solutions prior to progressing to mediation.
  • Administering a small business dispute mediation process.
  • Delivery type: Phone services
  • Service area: All of Queensland.
