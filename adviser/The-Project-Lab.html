Enterprise Centres Tasmania

The Project Lab

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

The Project Lab provides small businesses in Dorset, Flinders, George Town, Launceston, Meander Valley, Northern Midlands and West Tamar with advice to help them build and develop sustainable businesses.

Small businesses that employ less than 20 people can get advice on:

  • surviving through hard times
  • managing business finance
  • starting a new business
  • setting business objectives
  • writing business plans
  • developing a marketing strategy
  • digital ready skills
  • growing a business
  • succession planning

They have offices located in Hobart and Launceston.

  • Delivery type: Face to face, Phone services
  • Service area: Dorset, Flinders, George Town, Launceston, Meander Valley, Northern Midlands and West Tamar.
