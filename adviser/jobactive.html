Federal Government

jobactive

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

jobactive provides businesses with access to free information and services to help them advertise job vacancies and recruit staff.

Businesses can access the jobactive service to:

  • find job seekers based on criteria set in your advertisement
  • advertise job vacancies for free without approval delays
  • find a jobactive provider to help find the right candidates
  • increase online visitors to job vacancies
  • access an employer dashboard to track each job vacancy and manage candidates.
  • Delivery type: Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: Australia wide.
