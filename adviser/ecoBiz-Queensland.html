Queensland State Government

ecoBiz Queensland

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

ecoBiz Queensland provides Queensland businesses with free advice, training and workshops on ways to improve their environmental sustainability and lower operating costs.

This free program helps businesses understand and save on their energy, water and waste costs.

Businesses can access free one-on-one coaching with an expert sustainability consultant who will provide an action plan outlining cost saving measures.

ecoBiz also offers training, events and webinars covering various aspects of energy efficiency strategies.

  • Delivery type: Mobile services, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: All of Queensland.
