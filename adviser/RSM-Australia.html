Business Local

RSM Australia

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

RSM Australia provides intenders, start-ups and existing small businesses across the Wheatbelt, Mid West, Gascoyne and Pilbara regions in WA with free to low-cost advice, support, training and mentoring to help them build and develop sustainable businesses.

They have a team of local business advisers who understand the challenges small businesses face in these regions.

The advisers have a range of skills and expertise across a variety of industries.

New and existing businesses may access a range of services and advice, including:

  • planning for your business
  • growing your business through use of the web
  • taxation and financial matters
  • mentoring
  • business skills workshops
  • free resources, tools and templates
  • marketing strategies
  • website planning
  • staffing and HR issues

RSM have offices located in Narrogin, Corrigin, Jurien Bay, Moora, Merredin, Northam, Geraldton and Karratha. Advisers also regularly travel throughout the regions to run workshops and provide one-on-one consultation sessions.

They also have an online digital adviser who is available to offer expert advice via Skype, phone and email.

  • Delivery type: Face to face, Mobile services, Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: Wheatbelt, Mid West, Gascoyne, Pilbara.
