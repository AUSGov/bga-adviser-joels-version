Digital Ready for Business

Last Updated: 22 September 2021

Digital Ready for Business provides Tasmanian businesses with a free 4-hour digital coaching session with an expert digital coach.

Coaching sessions will be delivered remotely via Zoom or phone.

Topics can include anything regarding getting businesses online, including:

  • websites
  • e-commerce
  • booking systems
  • social media
  • SEO
  • content
  • advertising
  • online tools.

Tasmanian business owners, managers, marketers and representatives are invited to participate. Businesses must be registered with an Australian business number (ABN).

  • Delivery type: Online services, Phone services
  • Service area: Tasmania.
